# Advanced Derivatives - Pricing and Hedging Project

## Project Overview
This project, completed as part of the **MSc in Financial Engineering at EDHEC Business School**, focuses on the implementation of advanced derivatives pricing models and risk management strategies.

## Key Features
* **Numerical Methods:** Implementation of Monte Carlo simulations and Finite Difference Methods.
* **Models:** Application of the Black-Scholes framework and extensions (e.g., Stochastic Volatility or Jumps, depending on your specific work).
* **Greeks & Hedging:** Calculation of Delta, Gamma, and Vega to design a dynamic hedging strategy.
* **Python Implementation:** Use of `NumPy`, `SciPy`, and `Matplotlib` for financial modeling and visualization.

## Deliverables
* `ADVANCED_DERIVATIVES_PROJECT.ipynb`: Complete Python code with simulations.
* `ADVANCED_DERIVATIVES_REPORT.pdf`: Detailed financial analysis and theoretical background.
